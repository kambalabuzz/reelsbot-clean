Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 25, 2024

Your Privacy Matters: This Privacy Policy explains how ViralPilot collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Username or display name
  • Password (encrypted and securely stored)
  • Account preferences and settings

1.2 Social Media Integration Data

When you connect social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube), we collect and store:

  • OAuth Access Tokens: Securely encrypted tokens that allow us to post on your behalf
  • Refresh Tokens: To maintain connection without requiring repeated logins
  • Profile Information: Your username, profile ID, and avatar from connected platforms
  • Scope Permissions: Record of what permissions you've granted (e.g., video.upload)

1.3 Content and Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service:

  • Video topics, scripts, and generated content
  • Customization preferences (voice, art style, caption style, etc.)
  • Upload history and publishing logs
  • Series configurations and schedules
  • API usage and error logs

1.4 Technical Data

  • IP address and geolocation (country/region)
  • Device type and browser information
  • Usage analytics and performance metrics
  • Error reports and debugging logs

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Functions

  • Video Generation: Process your inputs to create AI-generated videos
  • Social Media Publishing: Use OAuth tokens to post videos to your connected accounts
  • Content Management: Store and organize your video series and schedules
  • Authentication: Verify your identity and maintain secure access

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Debug errors and optimize performance
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Monitor system health and prevent abuse

2.3 Communication

  • Send service updates and important notifications
  • Respond to support requests
  • Notify about account activity or security issues
  • Share product updates (with option to opt-out)

3. How We Store and Protect Your Data

3.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase (PostgreSQL): Account data, series configurations, and metadata
  • Supabase Storage: Generated videos and media files
  • Encrypted at Rest: All sensitive data encrypted in database
  • Encrypted in Transit: HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission

3.2 Security Measures

  • OAuth tokens encrypted with industry-standard encryption (AES-256)
  • Passwords hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication for all API endpoints
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse and brute-force attacks

3.3 Data Retention

  • Account data retained while your account is active
  • Generated videos stored according to your subscription plan
  • Logs and analytics data retained for 90 days
  • Upon account deletion, personal data removed within 30 days

4. Sharing Your Information

4.1 Third-Party Services We Use

We share limited data with trusted third parties to provide the Service:

  • TikTok: Video files and metadata when you authorize posting
  • Instagram: Video URLs and captions when you authorize posting
  • YouTube: Video files and metadata when you authorize posting
  • OpenAI: Text prompts for script generation (no personal identifiers)
  • ElevenLabs: Text scripts for voice synthesis (no personal identifiers)
  • Supabase: Database and file storage provider
  • Stripe: Payment processing (if using paid features)

4.2 What We DON'T Share

  • We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • We don't share your OAuth tokens with anyone
  • We don't use your content for training AI models without permission
  • We don't share analytics data that identifies individual users

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service
  • Protect rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Update Information: Modify your account details and preferences
  • Delete Your Account: Permanently remove your account and associated data
  • Revoke OAuth Access: Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Export Your Content: Download videos you've created

5.2 Social Media Permissions

You can manage social media permissions by:

  • Disconnecting accounts in your dashboard settings
  • Revoking access directly in TikTok/Instagram/YouTube settings
  • Choosing which platforms to publish to for each video series

5.3 Cookie Preferences

We use cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analytics (can be disabled in settings)

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify you via email of significant changes
  • Post updates on our website with revision date
  • Give you opportunity to review before continuing use

9. TikTok-Specific Privacy Information

When you connect your TikTok account:

  • We request only the minimum permissions needed (user.info.basic, video.upload)
  • We store your TikTok access token securely encrypted in our database
  • We only access your TikTok account when you explicitly authorize video uploads
  • We do not access your TikTok messages, followers, or other private data
  • You can revoke our access anytime in TikTok Settings > Security and login > Apps

10. Instagram-Specific Privacy Information

When you connect your Instagram Business account:

  • Requires linking to a Facebook Page (Instagram API requirement)
  • We request instagram_basic and instagram_content_publish permissions
  • We use long-lived tokens (60-day expiry) to maintain connection
  • We only upload videos you explicitly authorize
  • You can disconnect via Instagram Settings or Facebook App Settings

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data:

  • Email: privacy@aivideobot.com
  • Support: support@aivideobot.com
  • Data Protection Requests: dpo@aivideobot.com

We will respond to privacy inquiries within 30 days.

12. Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
